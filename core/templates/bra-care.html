Washing

Handwash

If you can handwash your bra, then go for it!
Make sure to use light detergentwhen possible.

Why handwash?

You have more controll over the treatment of your bra
like how hard or soft it is scrubbed/spun, what other
colored clothing it comes in contact with.

In the washing machine in a bra bag or pillow case.

Let's be real: not everyone has the time to handwash their bras.
That is okay! Putting your bra in a bra bag or pillow case means
there will be less wear and tear on the bra from other peices of
clothing. Hooks, lace and straps can easily get caught on other
peices of clothing so putting a bra in a bra bag or pillow case
takes away the possibility of damges.

Drying

NEVER put your bra in the dryer!

Even if your bra is wireless a dryer will put more wear on your bra
and will give it a shorter life span. It can lose it's shape quickly, and we
want this bra to last as long as it can.

How should I dry my bra then?

Hang it up or lay it flat. Hanging a bra up to dry it allows for more airflow
than if it were laying flat, but either option is a great way to dry your bra.

I just washed my bra and have an event how do I dry it quickly?

This is totally your call, it is your bra! If you have a hair dryer you can hair dry the cup, or put it in the dryer on the delicate cycle. We just suggest you don't use the dryer everytime you wash your bra!

Storage

There are two best ways to store your bra. One fo the biggest things is to not fold it this causes cups and frames deform over time.

Let it hangout!   or   Let it sleep!

Give your bra some time hang out.
We all need hangout time after support others all day! Connect the hooks to the loosest set of eyes on the band so the bra is relaxed as it hangs out.

Make a space for your bra in one drawer. Lay it flat so that other clothing items are not impacting it.
