Band Measurement

This is not your band size. Crazy right! Bra fitting is a funny thing indeed because a band size is actually calculated by adding a few more inches to your band measurement.
This number is a tool to find your best support not a definition of who you are!
{{ form.band_measurement }}
  • If your band measurement was an odd number we will add 5 inches to get your band size.
  • If your band measurement was and even number we will add 4 inches to get your band measurement.

Bust Measurement

Now in order to get your cup size you will need to do one more measurement. Pick which breast you think needs the most support. You will measure that side only by using one hand to hold the measuring tape in the middle of your back. You will then bring the tape to the middle of the front of your chest with your other hand. Make sure the tape remains parallel to the ground and is measuring the fullest part of your breast. Mark that measurement. This measurement is another tool we'll use to get the best support.
If you measured all the way around your bust that's fine!
