Welcome to Support Me!

Support Me is to SUPPORT YOU on your search for the best fitting bra. No matter who you are or what your story is, you are welcome here!

About 60% of people who wear bras are wearing the wrong size. This can lead to physical discomfort.

There is one out there and we will give you the information that you need in order to find it.

What is a bra size even?

Next we need to get a few other materials to help with the fitting process: Measuring tape or a string and ruler.

A bra size is usually shown as two numbers followed by a letter. The two numbers represent a band size and the letter represents a cup size. There are a few steps that we will take to get these values. Yes, calculations are involved. Yes, measurements are involved. These measurements are taken so you can find the bra size that will give you the most support.

These measurements do not define how amazing you are or your value as a person (that value is uncalculateable because you are too awesome for numbers!).
